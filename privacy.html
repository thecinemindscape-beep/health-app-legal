Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2026

✓ We do NOT collect personal data

Overview

This Privacy Policy describes how your information is handled when you use our health and wellness mobile application ("the App"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our practices.

Information We Don't Collect

We want to be clear: we do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. Specifically:

  • We do not collect your name, email, or contact information
  • We do not track your location
  • We do not collect device identifiers
  • We do not use analytics or tracking tools
  • We do not share any data with third parties

Data Storage

All data you enter in the App (such as water intake, health metrics, goals, and preferences) is stored locally on your device only. This data never leaves your device unless you explicitly choose to:

  • Back up your device via iCloud or iTunes
  • Sync with Apple Health (if you enable this feature)
  • Export your data manually

Apple Health Integration

If you choose to connect the App with Apple Health:

  • The App may read and write health data (such as water intake) to Apple Health
  • This data is governed by Apple's privacy policies
  • You can revoke this access at any time in your device Settings
  • We never access this data ourselves - it stays between the App and Apple Health on your device

In-App Purchases

Purchases are processed entirely by Apple through the App Store. We do not have access to your payment information, Apple ID, or any billing details.

Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for children under 17 years of age. We do not knowingly collect any information from children.

Data Security

Since all data is stored locally on your device, the security of your data depends on the security of your device. We recommend:

  • Using a device passcode or biometric lock
  • Keeping your device's operating system up to date
  • Using the built-in backup features of your device

Your Rights

You have complete control over your data:

  • Access: All your data is visible within the App
  • Export: You can export your data at any time
  • Delete: You can delete all data through the App's Settings
  • Uninstall: Removing the App will delete all associated data

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected in the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page.
